talking to machines

Machines are our faithful companions. More and more of our lives is spent in the company of our laptops, tablets and phones. But during this time, we've all been very rude. Computers are servants we depend on but never acknowledge. Most of us have never introduced ourselves to our machines, let alone engaged them in a conversation.

We can't go on like this. The best relationships are founded on communication, so we'd better start talking to each other.

The good news is that it isn't so difficult. As foreign languages go, there are far worse. In fact, the hardest step might just be to break the awkward silence.

So take the initiative. Write your name between the quotation marks and say hello.
